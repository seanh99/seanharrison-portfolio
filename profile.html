Profile

Sean Harrison is an architect and project lead based in Madrid, with experience across residential, hospitality, institutional and cultural projects in Europe, the Americas and the Caribbean. His work spans concept design, architectural development, BIM coordination, procurement and construction delivery. This range allows him to move between design intent and technical resolution, maintaining continuity from the first idea through to completion.

Sean trained at the Facultad de Arquitectura, Diseño y Urbanismo of the Universidad de Buenos Aires, graduating in 2016, and completed an exchange at the École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture Paris-Malaquais. He later undertook further studies in Project Management at the Universidad Tecnológica Nacional.

His professional experience includes residential projects in Argentina; refurbishment and hospitality work across Spain, France and the United Kingdom; and four years producing coordinated BIM models and construction documentation for civic and educational projects in the United States. He currently works in Madrid as a Lead Architect, directing residential and hospitality projects from concept through construction across Europe, Latin America and the Caribbean.

Alongside his professional work, Sean develops independent architectural studies and collaborations, with a particular interest in residential design, hospitality, visual storytelling and emerging design technologies.
