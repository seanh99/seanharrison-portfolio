Finca Son Ponent

Serra de Tramuntana, Mallorca, Spain · 2025
Harrison Jones · architectural concept, historic renovation and extension, interior design, landscape strategy and visual direction
725 m²

Finca Son Ponent transforms a historic Mallorcan possessió into a private home rooted in the terraces of the Tramuntana. The original stone house retains its weight and domestic character, while a low contemporary extension is partially embedded into the landscape. Local limestone, lime render, aged timber and deep thresholds establish a quiet continuity between old and new, with the Mediterranean remaining a distant presence beyond the olive groves.
