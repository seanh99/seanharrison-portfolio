Sean Harrison is an independent architecture practice working across residential, hospitality and cultural projects in Europe, the Americas and the Caribbean.

The practice leads each commission from early concept through developed and technical design, tender documentation, and construction-phase supervision — coordinating architecture, interiors and delivery as one continuous process rather than separate handoffs.

Every project responds to its own specific conditions: site, climate, material, and the particular aspirations of the people who will live in it.

We work in restraint. Careful proportion, honest materials and considered light take precedence over ornament — architecture that feels inevitable rather than designed.

Across luxury villas, heritage-sensitive refurbishments and civic buildings, the same discipline applies throughout: coordinated, buildable information, produced with the same care as the idea it protects.

Sean Harrison leads an independent architecture practice with more than a decade of experience delivering residential, institutional and cultural projects across Europe, the Americas and the Caribbean.

He trained at FADU, Universidad de Buenos Aires, graduating in 2016, with an exchange year at the École Nationale Supérieure d'Architecture Paris-Malaquais, and later completed further studies in Project Management at UTN in 2020.

Early in his career, Sean worked across zoning, feasibility and on-site supervision for residential projects in Argentina, before joining Beriot+Bernardini Arquitectos, where he directed refurbishments of residential properties across Spain, France and the UK — including the remodel of a historic château in Bordeaux into a hotel and winery.

He subsequently spent four years as a BIM Architect at Cordogan Clark Associates in the United States, producing coordinated Revit models and construction documentation for civic and educational projects, before returning to residential and hospitality work as Senior Architect and Project Lead at EFC Holdings, where he now leads concept-through-construction delivery on luxury villas across the Caribbean, Europe and Latin America.

Sean is fluent in Spanish, English and French, and has a working knowledge of Italian. Alongside his architectural practice, he maintains a close focus on advanced visualization and the pragmatic use of AI-assisted workflows in design and documentation.
